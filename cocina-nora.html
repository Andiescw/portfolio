La Cocina de Nora


La Cocina de Nora is a homemade dessert brand specializing in empanadas and cookies for events or custom orders. Nora, the owner, wanted a visual identity that reflected the artisanal and homemade nature of her products, lovingly crafted using her grandmother's traditional recipe.

We chose a feminine and simple a esthetic, much like her, using pink and red tones to evoke the tradition in her recipes, along with a cursive font that reinforces the personal and welcoming feel of the brand."
