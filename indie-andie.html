Indie Andie Creative Studio


Indie Andie was born out of the need for an identity under which I could undertake freelance projects without the pressure of using my own name, and eventually, to expand this initiative. I aimed to evoke a sense of creativity and professionalism through defined graphics and colors that allude to the creative aspect of a graphic design studio.

Additionally, I wanted to infuse a hint of my personality—feminine and vibrant—into the brand's visual representation, ensuring it reflects both my individuality and the ethos of the design industry.
